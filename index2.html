About the business

With the love of building track cars, came the duty for car maintenance and tuning. Custom modification became second nature. Coupled with the integral skills experienced in the aerospace industry, this solo “Monkey” took on the path to build custom guitars.

After successfully completing several intense hands-on courses in building acoustic/classical guitars, this Monkey continued the repertoire with repairing guitars. With the ambition to become the best and finest, the ultimate goal is to become a “luthier in training” once 200 guitars have been custom made by Monkey Guitars.

The fully equipped state-of-the-art wood shop houses a variety of eclectic woods from all over the world. This humidity-controlled work space offers the ideal climate for any custom manufacture guitar to be built. The adjoining music room with work bench is designated for guitar set-ups and repairs. Each and every guitar will be serviced one by one with the utmost attention and care.
